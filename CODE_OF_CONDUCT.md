# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming experience
for everyone, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Personal attacks or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Any other conduct that would reasonably be considered unprofessional

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards.
Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to kaneshir97006@gmail.com.
All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
